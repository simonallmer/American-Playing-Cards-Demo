2. Round Structure

The game lasts 5 Rounds. Hand capacity scales up by 1 card each round.

  1. Turns: Players take turns to bet. You must CALL, RAISE, or FOLD.
  2. Resolution: After the last turn, the best hand takes the Pot.
  3. Discard & Draw: After each round, played cards are discarded. Keep your unplayed hand and draw to reach capacity.

3. Victory & Tie-Breakers

Winner is the player with the Most Cash at the end of Round 5.

  • Tier 1: 5 of a Rank, Pure Suit, Coalition, or Campaign
  • Tier 2: 4 of a Rank, Pure, Coalition, or Campaign
  • Tier 3: 3 of a Rank, Pure, Coalition, or Campaign
  • Tier 4: Pairs
  • Tier 5 (Skirmishes): Unmatched cards.
